India defeated Australia in a thrilling cricket match held in Hyderabad on Sunday. 
Virat Kohli scored a magnificent century and played a crucial role in securing victory for the team. 
Rohit Sharma provided strong support with an impressive half-century. 
The bowlers also performed exceptionally well and restricted Australia to a modest total. 
The captain praised the team's effort and dedication throughout the tournament. 
Fans celebrated the victory across the country and shared their excitement on social media platforms. 
Cricket experts described the performance as one of the best displays of teamwork this season. 
Analysts believe the team's confidence will increase ahead of upcoming international matches. 
The coaching staff expressed satisfaction with the players' discipline and commitment. 
The victory helped India strengthen its position in the tournament standings.
